With expense tracker we help you in keeping a record of your daily expenses so that you can plan your finances, never go over the budget or break your head trying to remember an expense you forgot which can really mess up the balance sheet.





Expense tracker is an open source application created in 2023. It is inspired by the app "Day to Day Expenses" and created as a portfolio project.
